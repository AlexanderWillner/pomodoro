Pomodoro

Pomodoro is an antiprocrastination application that helps in Getting Things Done. It is a simple but effective way to manage your time and to boost your productivity to higher levels. Can be used for programming, studying, writing, cooking or simply concentrating on something important.

You can find more informations on the Pomodoro Technique on http://www.pomodorotechnique.com/
